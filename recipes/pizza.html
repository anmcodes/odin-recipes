Ingredients

  • 16 oz of pizza dough
  • 5 oz of pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzerella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of mexican blend cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1/2 can of diced pinapple
  • Pepperoni
  • Sliced Tomato

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Add layer of flower to pan
  3. Place dough on pan and let proof for 30 minutes
  4. Roll out pizza dough
  5. Add pizza sauce
  6. Add cheese
  7. Add toppings of your choice
  8. Melt butter
  9. Add minced garlic to melted butter
  10. Spread butter & garlic on crust
  11. Place in oven for 15 - 20 minutes
