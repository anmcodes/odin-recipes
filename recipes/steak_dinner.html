Ingredients

  • 12 oz New York Strip Steak
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cracked pepper
  • 1 tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup frozen vegetables
  • 1 potato

Steps

  1. Mix steak seasonings
  2. Prep steak
  3. Heat up pan with oil
  4. Add seasoned steak to pan
  5. Add butter to pan
  6. Cook steak to your preference
  7. Add frozen vegetables to seperate pan with olive oil
  8. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees
  9. Wash potato
  10. Poke holes around potato using a fork
  11. Cover potato in butter
  12. Lightly season potato with salt
  13. Place potato in foil
  14. Place potato in oven for 60 minutees
  15. Enjoy
