Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablesppoon flax seeds
  • 1/4 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup almond milk

Steps

  1. Get a small mason jar
  2. Add 1/2 cup of oats
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of flax seeds
  5. Add 1/4 tablespoon of cinnamon
  6. Add 1/2 cup of almond milk
  7. Mix ingredients within mason jar
  8. Place mix in refrigerator overnight
  9. Add strawberries & blueberries to mix
  10. Stir well and enjoy
